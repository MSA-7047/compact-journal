About

What is Compact Journals?

Compact Journals is a web-based journalling application created using the Python Django framework.

This web application was built by 8 students from King's College London as part of their large group software engineering project for the module 5CCS2SEG. This group project took place during the second semester of academic year 2023-24.

Features

What can you do with Compact Journals?

Track your progress

Keep an eye on how you're doing each day.

Earn badges

Get rewarded with badges for writing in your journal every day

Write journals

Record your thoughts and experiences regularly.

Track your mood

Keep tabs on how you're feeling over the month.

Check previous journal entries

Take a peek at what you've written in the past.

Collaborate on group journals

Work together on shared journal entries with friends.
